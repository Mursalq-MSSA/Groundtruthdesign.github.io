Ground Truth Design

Ground Truth Design unlocks your teams’ full capacities to innovate and follow through, starting from the very first steps. We teach four Basic Skillsets: Human-Centered Design to find problems worth solving; Operational Experimentation to test actionable theories of improvement; Agile to bring the project to completion, capture lessons, and improve both future products and future processes; and Ecosystem Learning to surface and share new advantages. Our proprietary unified approach, called Tempo, ensures your teams ask the right questions, solve the right problems, deliver results, and continuously improve – together.

Our two-day remote classes introduce you to Scrum

an industry best-practice tool for running a high-performing team. Graduates receive a Scrum, Inc., “Registered Scrum Master” credential and become part of GTD’s alumni community. Buy by the seat here EVENTBRITE COLLECTION or buy a multi-seat package for your teams by reaching out to inquiries@groundtruth.design.

Agile Emplacement Course

A three-day, in-person Agile implementation workshop for up to 30 of your teammembers. Together, we inventory current work, establish roles and cadences for managing both current and new work, and get each team’s process up and running. Graduates receive ongoing coaching and become part of GTD’s alumni community. “Registered Scrum Master” credentials available upon request.

Comprehensive Basic Innovators Course

Through two remote days and up to five in-person days, your most promising innovators learn all of GTD’s Basic Skillsets by practicing on real, high-value challenges sourced from your organization. Includes a simultaneous six-hour session for your organization’s key leaders, so they can effectively employ your newly-minted innovators, and a detailed after-action report (AAR) on process, outcomes, and recommended next steps. All Basic Innovators receive a Registered Scrum Master credential from Scrum, Inc.; coaching, through AAR finalization; and membership in GTD’s alumni community.

Cohort Accelerator

“If you would go far, go together.” Help your cohort of fellows, teams, or companies get the most out of their time with you and each other. Through our workshops, they’ll radiate goals, needs, and assistance, seizing every chance to serve one another. And, with all their needs clearly laid out in realtime, you’ll be able to spot where they need guidance or a connection, helping to accelerate their progress during the limited time they have with you.

PSaaS

Often paired with our Cohort Accelerator, our “Portfolio-Support”-as-a-service helps venture capitalists and accelerators to offer the growth support their portfolio companies need without having to do it all themselves. Map common milestones like Category Identification, Minimum Viable Products, Minimum Viable Repeatability, and Minimum Viable Traction; combine high-talent individuals into high-performing teams, focused on their milestone goals; and apply the essentials of design, experimentation, and agile to accelerate time to learning, reduce risk, and shorten the space between each milestone.
